Wirecard

Wirecard AG is an insolvent German payment processor and financial services provider at the center of a financial scandal in Germany. The company offers[5] its customers electronic payment transaction services and risk management, as well as the issuing and processing of physical cards. In June 2020, the company reported €1.9 billion in cash missing. CEO Markus Braun resigned and was arrested. On June 25, 2020, Wirecard filed for insolvency and Michael Jaffé was confirmed as insolvency administrator.
